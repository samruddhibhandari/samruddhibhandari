## 👩‍💻 About Me

I'm a **final-year Information Technology student** at Sinhgad College of Engineering, Pune, who turns raw, messy data into decisions businesses can actually act on.

Instead of stopping at tutorials, I've built three end-to-end analytics projects from scratch — designing a 25,000+ row relational database, scraping and analyzing live job market data, and uncovering the real drivers behind employee attrition. Each one follows the same process I'd bring to a real internship: define the KPIs, clean and model the data, find the "why" behind the numbers, and present it in a dashboard a non-technical stakeholder could actually use.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📊 SkillScope — India's Data Science Job Market Intelligence Dashboard
> Python · BeautifulSoup · Pandas · NewsAPI · VADER · Power BI

**What it does:**
Scrapes 167 real data science job postings from Internshala, enriches them with company news sentiment via NewsAPI, performs complete EDA across 6 KPIs, and delivers insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

**Key Findings:**
- 📈 Experience (r=0.534) is the strongest salary predictor — far stronger than skill count (-0.21)
- 💰 AI roles average ₹11.38 LPA in high-tier positions; Data Engineers lead at ₹14.5L
- 🏠 Remote jobs pay ₹5.64L avg vs ₹5.39L for office — WFH is not a pay cut
- 📍 WFH dominates with 66 openings; Bangalore leads physical locations with 25
- 🔍 75.4% of companies disclose salary — higher transparency than expected

[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-SkillScope-2C5F8A?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/samruddhibhandari/SkillScope)

### 🚢 Port Congestion & Cargo Delay Intelligence System
> MySQL 8.0 · Python · Power BI · Window Functions · CTEs

**What it does:**
A 6-table normalized relational schema modeling ports, vessels, voyages, delays, and cargo manifests, analyzed with 28 SQL queries across 5 difficulty tiers to power a 7-KPI Power BI dashboard for stakeholder reporting.

**Key Findings:**
- ⚓ 83.51% cascade delay rate identified across 3,923 voyage pairs
- 🏗️ Infrastructure tier is the strongest delay predictor (4.05 vs 12.58 avg hrs)
- 💵 ~$35,000-per-voyage saving opportunity identified from rerouting through Tier 1 ports
- 📋 10 data-backed business recommendations delivered with full root-cause analysis

[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-Port%20Congestion-2C5F8A?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/samruddhibhandari/Port-Congestion-Cargo-Delay-Intelligence-System)

### 📉 IBM HR Analytics — Employee Attrition Dashboard
> Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Seaborn · Power BI

**What it does:**
Complete EDA on 1,470 employee records across 35 features to identify key attrition drivers and visualize patterns through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

**Key Findings:**
- ⚠️ Employees working overtime are 3x more likely to leave
- 💼 Low job satisfaction and low monthly income are top attrition predictors
- 📊 Interactive dashboard showing department-wise risk, salary distribution, and driver analysis
